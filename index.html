What is it?

Psychotherapy, simply

Also known as talk therapy or counselling, psychotherapy is a collaborative process between a therapist and a client. It provides a confidential space to openly discuss concerns, challenges, and goals — promoting self-awareness, emotional healing, and personal growth.

How it helps

Areas we can explore

  • Mental health: Depression, anxiety, trauma, stress and more.
  • Relationships: Communication, conflict, deeper connection.
  • Life transitions: Marriage, divorce, career, loss.
  • Self-exploration: Values, identity, meaning.
  • Behavioural change: Coping strategies and resilience.
