About me

Discover Healing
Through Psychotherapy

At Nirupama Inchalmath Psychotherapy, I believe in the profound capacity of therapy to impact lives. My practice offers a safe, supportive environment where individuals can explore their thoughts, feelings, and experiences with complete openness.

I am a qualified Integrative Counsellor and Psychotherapist, and a registered member of the British Association for Counselling and Psychotherapy (BACP), adhering to its ethical framework. I offer an integrative approach drawing on Cognitive Behavioural Therapy, Attachment Theory, and Person-Centred Approach, as well as ACT, Compassion Focussed Therapy, and DBT.

I perceive therapy as a collaborative journey navigating the depths of human experience with both care and clinical rigour. I embrace an ecological, biopsychosocial standpoint and have worked with people from the East to the West, across varied cultural backgrounds, all genders, sexualities, and age groups. I offer therapy in Hindi and Kannada within the UK.

My approach

Philosophy

My approach is rooted in deep respect for the unique journey of each individual. No two people are alike — so every therapeutic relationship is tailored to the specific needs and goals of the person in front of me.

  1. 01

    What is Psychotherapy?

    A collaborative process between therapist and client — a confidential space to openly discuss concerns, challenges, and goals. Through dialogue and exploration, psychotherapy promotes self-awareness, emotional healing, and personal growth.

  2. 02

    Holistic Healing

    I believe in addressing the interconnectedness of mind, body, and environment. Taking a holistic approach means considering every dimension of your well-being to promote comprehensive healing — not merely symptom relief.

  3. 03

    Collaborative Partnership

    You are the expert on your own life. I value open dialogue, mutual respect, and shared decision-making. Therapy works best when your self-knowledge meets my clinical expertise in equal measure.

  4. 04

    Strengths-Based Perspective

    Each individual possesses unique strengths, resources, and resilience. My role is to help you identify and leverage those inherent strengths — to overcome challenges rather than focus solely on deficit.

  5. 05

    Culturally-Informed Practice

    I am committed to culturally competent, inclusive care — honouring the diverse backgrounds, identities, and experiences of every client. Equality, Diversity and Inclusion is central to my practice, guided by the Equality Act 2010.

  6. 06

    Evidence-Based & Long-Term Growth

    I integrate evidence-based practices supported by research and clinical expertise, staying current through continuous professional development. The goal is not only to alleviate symptoms — it is to foster lasting resilience and the capacity to navigate life with confidence.
