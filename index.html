What is Psychotherapy ?

Psychotherapy, also known as talk therapy or counselling, is a collaborative process between a therapist and a client. It provides a confidential space where individuals can openly discuss their concerns, challenges, and goals. Through dialogue and exploration, psychotherapy aims to promote self-awareness, emotional healing, and personal growth.

Philosophy

At Nirupama Inchalmath Psychotherapy, my approach to psychotherapy is rooted in a deep respect for the unique journey of each individual. I understand that no two people are alike, and as such, I tailor the therapeutic approach to meet the specific needs and goals of my clients. My philosophy is guided by several core principles

Holistic Healing

I believe in addressing the interconnectedness of mind, body and environment in the healing process. As a therapist I take a holistic approach, considering the various dimensions of your well-being to promote comprehensive healing and growth.

Collaborative Partnership

Therapy is a collaborative process between therapist and client. I value your expertise in your own life and encourage open dialogue, mutual respect, and shared decision-making throughout the therapeutic journey.

Strengths-Based Perspective

I recognize that each individual possesses unique strengths, resources, and resilience. As a therapist, I strive to empower clients by identifying and leveraging their inherent strengths to overcome challenges and achieve their goals.

Culturally-Informed Practice

I am committed to providing culturally competent and inclusive care. I honour the diverse backgrounds, identities, and experiences of my clients and strive to create a safe and affirming space for all individuals, regardless of race, ethnicity, gender identity, sexual orientation, or religious beliefs. Equality, Diversity and Inclusion (EDI) is at the heart of my practice whilst being considerate of the Equality Act 2010.

Evidence-Based Interventions

I integrate evidence-based practices and therapeutic techniques supported by research and clinical expertise. I continuously stay informed about the latest advancements in the field of psychology and psychotherapy to ensure the highest quality of care for our clients. I am committed to continuous professional development (CPD).

Long-Term Growth and Resilience

The goal is not only to alleviate symptoms but also to foster long-term growth, resilience, and well-being. I work with clients to develop sustainable coping strategies, enhance self-awareness, and cultivate the skills needed to navigate life's challenges with confidence and resilience.

Compassionate Support

Above all, we provide a compassionate and nonjudgmental space where clients feel heard, understood, and accepted. Our therapists offer genuine empathy, support, and encouragement as clients explore their thoughts, feelings, and experiences in a safe and nurturing environment.
